    Your answer to guitar lessons is here...

    Craig is an experienced and vastly knowledgeable musician and teacher with a degree in music that has offered instructive services in Clacton On Sea and surrounding areas since 2010. Alongside guitar tuition, he is also a professional performer and composer. He is often commended for his skill and versatility as not only a guitarist but in equal measure as a patient tutor who knows how to teach at his student's pace. Whether you're just starting out or you're an advancing player looking to expand your ability and theory knowledge, Craig's aim is to identify your goals and help you reach them by providing the technical guidance and fundamental skills you need to unlock your potential.

    With over 15 years experience as a musician, Craig is proficient in playing and teaching a wide range of styles including Pop, Rock, Blues, Funk, Jazz, Soul, R&B, Reggae, Metal, Gospel, Country, Folk, Classical and more. Whatever your musical preference, his relaxed and 'easy to follow' approach to teaching is sure to prove valuable to beginners and advanced players alike. With all lessons being tailored to the individual needs of the student, Craig makes sure that learning is always constructive, inspiring and fun!
